## Table
The hardest part in my latex journey.
```
\begin{table}[h!]
\caption{Sample table title. This is where the description of the table should go.}
      \begin{tabular}{cccc}
        \hline
           & B1  &B2   & B3\\ \hline
        A1 & 0.1 & 0.2 & 0.3\\
        A2 & ... & ..  & .\\
        A3 & ..  & .   & .\\ \hline
      \end{tabular}
\end{table}
```
